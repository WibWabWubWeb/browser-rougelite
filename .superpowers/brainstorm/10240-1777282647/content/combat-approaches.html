Backline Participation

If only the front units fight 1v1, what do the units behind them do on the timeline?

A

Pure Reserve

Units only enter the timeline when they reach the front. The fight is a strict 1v1 until someone dies, then the next unit "tags in."

B

Active Support

Everyone is on the timeline. Backline units can't attack directly, but they can use turns to Heal, Buff the leader, or Swap places to the front.

C

Rotating Duel

The unit at the front attacks and then automatically moves to the back of the line, cycling the next unit forward. The "1v1" is constantly rotating.
