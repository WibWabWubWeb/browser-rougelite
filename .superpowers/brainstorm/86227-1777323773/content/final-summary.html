Final Design Summary: Random Events

Choice-driven narrative encounters with transparent outcomes

1. Narrative Interface

A full-screen cinematic overlay (similar to the Shop) showing a textual prompt and 2-3 interactive choice cards.

"A solar storm is approaching. Your shields are flickering. What is your command?"
OVERCLOCK SHIELDS
EFFECT: 100% Protection
COST: 20 Credits
BRACE FOR IMPACT
EFFECT: -10 HP to Squad
COST: Free

2. Event Mechanics

  • Variety: Events can provide Credits, Items, Squad Healing, or Unit Upgrades.
  • Risks: Some choices may have a probability of failure, clearly shown on the button (e.g., "50% Chance to Fail").
  • Transparency: Every choice explicitly lists its immediate effects so players can make strategic trade-offs.
