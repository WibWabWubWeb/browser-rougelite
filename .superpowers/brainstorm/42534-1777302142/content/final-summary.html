Final Design Summary: Polish & Pace

Visible networks, specialized threats, and high-speed combat

1. Full Network Visibility

All forward connections are rendered as subtle SVG lines, revealing the full map structure. Reachable paths are highlighted with animated dashes.

2. Specialized Enemy Squads

Enemy encounters share a single Armor Type (e.g., "The Plating Fleet"). You know what defense to break, but their weapons (ATK) remain randomized and dangerous.

3. Combat Pacing Control

A persistent speed bar during duels. Toggle between 1x, 2x, 4x speeds, or use SKIP to instantly resolve the battle based on the current math.
