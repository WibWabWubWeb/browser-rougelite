Combat Approaches: How the lanes work

Choosing the core "Tactical Puzzle" for the player

1. The Battle Line (Single Lane)

Units are lined up in a single row. The front units fight until one dies, then the next steps up.

  • Simple to learn
  • Focus on unit order
  • Lower tactical depth

Example: Super Auto Pets

2. The 3-Lane Siege (Recommended)

Three distinct lanes. Units fight their direct counterparts. Winning a lane deals damage to the enemy "Base".

  • Strong counter-play
  • Split-focus strategy
  • Slightly more UI work

Example: Artifact / Card Game style

3. Shifting Frontlines

5 lanes. Units move to help adjacent lanes if they win their own battle early.

  • Very dynamic
  • Feels like a "battle"
  • Harder to predict
