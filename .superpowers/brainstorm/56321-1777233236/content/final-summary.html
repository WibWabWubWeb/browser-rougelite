Final Design Summary: Sci-Fi Squad Roguelite

A tactical auto-battler for the browser

Project Blueprint

⚔️ Combat: 3-Lane Siege

Assign troops to Top, Center, or Bottom lanes. Battles are automated. Win lanes to damage the enemy base.

🧬 Logic: The Star Cycle

6 Types (Thermal, Ion, Toxic vs. Plating, Shields, Bio). Every unit has clear hard-counters.

🗺️ Map: The Sector Web

Branching paths through space nodes: Skirmishes, Elites, Trade Hubs (Shops), and Distress Signals.

📈 Growth: Hybrid Leveling

Units gain simple Stat boosts (ATK/HP) most levels, with Milestone ability choices at specific ranks.
