How do you acquire new troops?

Deciding the core loop for building your squad

A

Shop / Drafting

Spend currency (Credits) to buy units from a rotating shop. Can refresh the shop for a fee. Similar to Teamfight Tactics or Super Auto Pets.

B

Post-Battle Rewards

After each victory, choose one of three random troop options to add to your roster for free. Similar to Slay the Spire or Balatro.
