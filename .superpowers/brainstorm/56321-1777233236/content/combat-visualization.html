How do the auto-battles look and feel?

Defining the core "automated fight" visualization and mechanics

Option A: Lane-Based (Tug-of-War)

Units are placed in specific lanes (e.g., Top, Middle, Bottom). They fight whoever is directly in front of them.

  • Simple: Very easy to predict counters.
  • Focus: Positioning units in the right lane to counter specific enemies.
Option B: Free-Form Grid (Auto-Chess)

Units are placed on a grid. Once the fight starts, they move and target enemies based on their range/AI.

  • Dynamic: More visual chaos and exciting "moments".
  • Focus: Tactical placement (Frontline vs. Backline).
A

Lane-Based
