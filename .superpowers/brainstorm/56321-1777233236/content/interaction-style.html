How do the 5-6 types interact?

Defining the depth of the combat system

Option A: The Star Cycle

Each type has 2 strengths and 2 weaknesses in a continuous web.

  • Laser beats Armor & Shields
  • Plasma beats Laser & Bio
  • Kinetic beats Plasma & Shields
  • ...and so on.

Best for: Clear "counter-picking" strategy.

Option B: Role-Based Synergies

Types represent combat roles rather than just damage types.

  • Vanguard (Tank): Absorbs damage.
  • Infiltrator (Assassin): Targets backline.
  • Artillery (AoE): Massive slow damage.
  • Technician (Healer/Buffer)

Best for: Building "combos" and balanced squads.
