2. XP & Skill Trees

Units gain XP from battles. Leveling up grants stat points or a choice between two abilities.

  • High attachment to units
  • Custom builds
  • More "menu" management

3. Modular Upgrades

Units have 1-2 "Module Slots". You find items (e.g., "Turbo Charger", "Reflective Hull") to customize them.

  • Flexible strategy
  • Easy to understand
  • Relies on item RNG
