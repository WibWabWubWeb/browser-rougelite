Decoupled Unit Examples

How combinations create unique unit identities

"Magma Tank"

ATK: 🔥 Thermal DEF: 🛡️ Plating

Melt-specialist. Great at destroying armored enemies, but vulnerable to Ion fire.

"Static Hydra"

ATK: ⚡ Ion DEF: 🌿 Bio

Utility fighter. Good at disabling shields, and its organic hull absorbs kinetic shock.

"Acid Ghost"

ATK: ☣️ Toxic DEF: 🌀 Shields

Fast harasser. Dissolves biological hulls while its energy fields deflect physical rounds.
