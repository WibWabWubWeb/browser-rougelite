Directions for Complexity

How should we differentiate the unit types beyond a simple loop?

A

Decoupled Attack/Armor

Every unit has an Attack Type (Thermal, Ion, Toxic) and an Armor Type (Plating, Shields, Bio). This creates 9 unique combinations per unit.

Unit: "Inferno Bastion"
ATK: 🔥 Thermal
DEF: 🛡️ Plating
B

Unique Mechanic Effects

Counters aren't just damage. ⚡ Ion might stall the enemy ATB bar, while ☣️ Toxic applies a damage-over-time "Corrosion" that weakens 🛡️ Plating armor over time.

⚡ ION hits 🌀 SHIELDS
-> EFFECT: ATB RESET (50%)
C

Nested/Branching Loop

Instead of 1-to-1, a type might counter two things but have two specific weaknesses. For example, 🌿 Bio resists ⚡ Ion and ☢️ Radiation, but is destroyed by 🔥 Thermal.
