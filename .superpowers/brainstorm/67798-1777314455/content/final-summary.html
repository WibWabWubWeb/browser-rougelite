Final Design Summary: The Trade Hub

A single-page dashboard for upgrades, repairs, and recruitment

1. Single Page Layout

The Trade Hub displays all available options at once, divided into three clear columns. Players can see their total credits and plan their purchases across different categories without navigating menus.

2. Inventory Mechanics

  • Consumables (30c): Bought and stored. Can be used from the Map screen to heal a specific unit.
  • Modules (80c): Bought and permanently attached to a unit. Max 1 module per unit initially.
  • Recruits (150c): Selecting a recruit forces you to choose a current squad member to "discharge", replacing them immediately.
